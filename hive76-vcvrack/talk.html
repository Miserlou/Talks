"Standard" Inputs and Outputs for Audio and Control Voltage
Control voltage carries information about how to modulate an effect on a sound
ex: Put an LFO to modulate the frequency of a filter
Audio: +/- 5V Control: +/- 10/12V
Eurorack: Volts VCVRack: Floats (numbers with a decimal point)
TL;DR: Get input, modulate it, send to output
